Crispy Vegetable Pakoras

Description

These pakoras with vegetables are wonderful. If you've ever tried Japanese tempura-coated vegetables, you'll adore this recipe. These pakoras are similar, but their distinctive flavor comes from the spicy batter.

Serve with sweet and sour, mint yogurt, or chili sauce. Other veggies including sweet potatoes, broccoli, and asparagus are good additions to this dish.
