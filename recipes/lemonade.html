Lemonade

Description

This classic homemade lemonade recipe will quench your thirst on a hot day with its perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess.

It's incredibly easy to make and requires just a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 large lemons
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices and mint leaves (optional garnish)

Steps

  1. Roll lemons to loosen juice.
  2. Cut in half and juice, then strain to remove seeds.
  3. Combine 1 cup sugar and 1 cup water in a saucepan.
  4. Heat, stirring, until sugar dissolves.
  5. Cool the syrup.
  6. In a pitcher, combine lemon juice and simple syrup.
  7. Add 3 cups cold water, stir, and taste.
  8. Adjust sweetness if needed.
